Know More About HealthMate Pharmacy Limited

HealthMate Pharmacy Limited is a patient-oriented pharmacy that considers the well-being of our patients and the satisfaction of our customers the utmost priority.Some of our services include but not limited to.

-Patient education and counseling.
-Drug information services.
-Dispensing and filling prescription.
-Medication Reconciliation services.
-Blood pressure,Blood glucose and Body Weight Measurements.
-Sell of Multivitamin Supplements for all age groups including weight loss suppliments.
-Pharmaceutical care services.
-Sell of Cosmetics and Groceries.
-Delivery of pharmaceutical and cosmetic products anywhere within and outside Lagos.
